<p><p>PCB laminate market types include glass fabric, epoxy resin, kraft paper, and phenolic resin. Glass fabric laminates are durable and have excellent electrical properties. Epoxy resin laminates offer high mechanical strength and good heat resistance. Kraft paper laminates provide flexibility and high insulation. Phenolic resin laminates are flame retardant and have good dimensional stability. These different types of PCB laminates cater to various applications and requirements within the electronics industry, offering a wide range of properties and benefits.</p></p>
