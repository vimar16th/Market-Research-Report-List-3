<p><p>Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) Coaters market includes three main types: Thermal Evaporation, Sputter Deposition, and Arc Vapor Deposition. Thermal Evaporation involves heating a solid material in a vacuum to generate a vapor that condenses onto a substrate. Sputter Deposition uses high-energy particles to dislodge atoms from a target material, which then deposit onto a substrate. Arc Vapor Deposition utilizes an electric arc to vaporize a target material, forming a coating on a substrate. These methods are widely used in industries such as electronics, optics, and automotive.</p></p>
