<p><ul><li>Microporous Materials (Less than 2 nm)</li><li>Mesoporous Materials (2-50 nm)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Meso-micro porous carbon materials are divided into two main categories based on their pore size: microporous materials with pores less than 2 nm in size, and mesoporous materials with pores ranging from 2 to 50 nm. Microporous materials are known for their high surface area and excellent adsorption properties, while mesoporous materials have larger pores that allow for faster mass transport. Both types of materials are important in various industries such as energy storage, gas separation, and catalysis due to their unique porosity and surface properties.</p></p>
