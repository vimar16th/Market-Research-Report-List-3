<p><ul><li>Single Common Anode</li><li>Dual Common Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrafast rectifier diodes are available in two main market types: single common anode and dual common anode. In single common anode diodes, all individual diodes share a common anode terminal, making them ideal for applications requiring high-speed rectification in a compact form factor. On the other hand, dual common anode diodes feature two separate common anode terminals, providing flexibility for more complex circuit designs and allowing for improved thermal management. Both types offer high-speed rectification capabilities, but cater to different needs based on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
