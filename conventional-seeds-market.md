<p><p>Conventional seeds market types refer to the different genetic traits engineered into seeds to provide specific benefits. Herbicide-tolerance (HT) seeds are designed to withstand certain herbicides, allowing for easier weed control. Insect-resistance (IR) seeds are genetically modified to produce proteins toxic to insect pests, reducing the need for chemical insecticides. Both HT and IR seeds offer farmers increased convenience and efficiency in crop management, leading to higher yields and potentially lower production costs.</p></p>
