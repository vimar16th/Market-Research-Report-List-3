<p><strong>The Automatic Mixer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gating</li><li>Gain Sharing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic mixer market types include Gating and Gain Sharing. Gating market type involves automatically adjusting the levels of different inputs based on specific parameters or thresholds. This ensures that only the desired signals are being mixed together. On the other hand, Gain Sharing market type involves automatically adjusting the gain of each input signal to achieve a balanced and clear output. Both types of automatic mixers help improve the efficiency and quality of audio mixing processes.</p></p>
