<p><strong>The Connected Vehicles Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>V2V</li><li>V2I</li><li>V2P</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Connected Vehicles Technology refers to the integration of vehicles with advanced communication systems to enhance safety, efficiency, and convenience. Three major market types exist: V2V (Vehicle-to-Vehicle), V2I (Vehicle-to-Infrastructure), and V2P (Vehicle-to-Pedestrian). V2V enables vehicles to directly exchange information, such as speed and position, to avoid accidents. V2I enables communication between vehicles and infrastructure, such as traffic signals, to optimize traffic flow. V2P facilitates communication between vehicles and pedestrians, enhancing pedestrian safety by notifying drivers of their presence. Ultimately, these technologies aim to create a more connected and cooperative transportation system.</p></p>
