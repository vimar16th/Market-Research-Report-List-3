<p><p>Synthetic feed antioxidants market types include BHT (butylated hydroxytoluene), BHA (butylated hydroxyanisole), Ethoxyquin, Propyl gallate, and others. BHT and BHA are commonly used as synthetic feed antioxidants to prevent oxidation in feed ingredients and maintain product quality. Ethoxyquin is another widely used antioxidant, known for its stability and effectiveness in preventing lipid oxidation. Propyl gallate is also used as a feed antioxidant, offering protection against rancidity. Other antioxidants in this market segment may include citric acid, ascorbic acid, and tocopherols.</p></p>
