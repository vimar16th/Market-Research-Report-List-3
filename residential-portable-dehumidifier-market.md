<p><ul><li>Compressor Type Dehumidifiers</li><li>Desiccant Type Dehumidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential Portable Dehumidifiers come in two main types: Compressor Type Dehumidifiers and Desiccant Type Dehumidifiers. Compressor Type Dehumidifiers work by drawing in air, cooling it to condense moisture, and then reheating the air before releasing it back into the room. On the other hand, Desiccant Type Dehumidifiers use a desiccant material to absorb moisture from the air, which is then collected and disposed of. Both types are effective in reducing humidity levels in residential spaces and improving indoor air quality.</p></p>
