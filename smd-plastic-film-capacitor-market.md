PEN capacitors offer high dielectric strength and temperature stability, making them suitable for high-performance applications. PET capacitors are known for their cost-effectiveness and good electrical properties. PPS capacitors provide excellent thermal and chemical resistance, making them ideal for harsh environments. Each type caters to different needs in the market, offering a range of performance characteristics for various electronic devices.</p></p>
