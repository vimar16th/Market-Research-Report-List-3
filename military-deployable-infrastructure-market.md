<p><ul><li>Soft Deployable Infrastructure</li><li>Hard Deployable Infrastructure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Military deployable infrastructure refers to the various systems and structures used by armed forces that can be quickly transported and set up in different locations. Soft deployable infrastructure includes lightweight and flexible components like tents, modular buildings, and inflatable structures, which are easily transportable and can be rapidly deployed. On the other hand, hard deployable infrastructure includes more robust and permanent structures such as prefabricated buildings, containerized units, and mobile command centers, designed for longer-term usage in challenging environments. Both types cater to the military's need for rapid establishment of operational bases and support facilities.</p></p>
