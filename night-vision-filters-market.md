<p><ul><li>Absorption Filtration</li><li>Laminated Filtration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Night vision filters can be categorized into two main types: Absorption Filtration and Laminated Filtration. Absorption Filtration involves utilizing materials that absorb certain wavelengths of light, allowing only specific frequencies to pass through, enhancing night vision capabilities in various conditions. On the other hand, Laminated Filtration involves combining multiple layers of materials with varying properties to enhance image clarity and contrast in low-light environments. Both market types cater to specific needs and preferences of consumers looking to enhance their night vision capabilities.</p></p>
