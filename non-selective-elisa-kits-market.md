<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Non-Selective ELISA Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct ELISA</li><li>Sandwich ELISA</li><li>Indirect ELISA</li><li>Competitive ELISA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-selective ELISA kits have different types, including Direct ELISA, Sandwich ELISA, Indirect ELISA, and Competitive ELISA. Direct ELISA involves detecting the antigen directly using a labeled antibody. Sandwich ELISA detects an antigen by sandwiching it between two antibodies, while Indirect ELISA uses a primary antibody followed by a labeled secondary antibody to detect the antigen. Competitive ELISA measures the competition between labeled and unlabeled antigens to determine antigen concentration. The market for these ELISA kits is diverse, offering various options for antigen detection in research and diagnostic applications.</p></p>
