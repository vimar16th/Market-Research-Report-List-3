<p><p>Medical infusion paste is a type of medication that is administered through the skin via a transdermal patch or an infusion device. It is specifically designed for patients who are unable to take medications orally or through injections. The paste is composed of various active ingredients that are slowly absorbed into the bloodstream, providing a controlled release of the medication over an extended period of time.</p><p>The market for medical infusion paste is projected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. One of the key drivers for this growth is the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, such as diabetes, cardiovascular diseases, and cancer. These conditions often require long-term medication and can benefit from the controlled release provided by medical infusion paste.</p><p>Another factor contributing to market growth is the growing geriatric population. Elderly individuals often have difficulties swallowing pills or injections, making medical infusion paste a convenient alternative.
