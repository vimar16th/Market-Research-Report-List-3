<p><p>Corrosion protective coatings and acid proof linings can be classified into three main market types based on their composition: solvent-borne, waterborne, and powder-based coatings. Solvent-borne coatings contain organic solvents that help in the application process and provide a durable finish. Waterborne coatings use water as a dispersing agent, making them more environmentally friendly. Powder-based coatings are applied as a dry powder and then cured with heat, resulting in a tough and resistant coating. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in various industries for corrosion protection.</p></p>
