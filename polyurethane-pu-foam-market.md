The PU foam market can be divided into two main types: rigid foam and flexible foam. Rigid foam is lightweight and provides high thermal insulation, making it ideal for construction and automotive applications. Both types of PU foam offer excellent comfort, durability, and energy efficiency benefits.</p></p>
