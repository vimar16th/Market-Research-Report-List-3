<p><p>The global Drowsing Driver Alert System (DDAS) is a technology designed to detect signs of drowsiness or fatigue in drivers and alert them to prevent accidents caused by a lack of attention. Once signs of drowsiness are detected, the system initiates an alarm or a warning to alert the driver, preventing potential accidents.</p><p>The DDAS market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period.
