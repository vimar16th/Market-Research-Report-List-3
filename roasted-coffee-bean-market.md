<p><p>The roasted coffee bean market is classified into three main types - light roast, medium roast, and dark roast. Light roast coffee beans are roasted for a shorter time, resulting in a light brown color and a milder flavor. Medium roast coffee beans have a slightly darker brown color and a more balanced taste, with a mix of acidity and body. Dark roast coffee beans are roasted for a longer time, resulting in a dark brown color and a stronger, bold flavor with lower acidity levels. These market types offer different taste profiles and cater to varying preferences of coffee drinkers.</p></p>
