<p><p>Expandable Graphite Sheets are categorized based on their expansion volume, measured in milliliters per gram (ml/g). Sheets below 150 ml/g have a low expansion capacity, making them suitable for applications where minimal expansion is required. Sheets between 150 ml/g and 350 ml/g have a moderate expansion capacity, ideal for applications needing a balance between expansion and material strength. Sheets above 350 ml/g have a high expansion capacity, making them suitable for applications where maximum expansion is needed for fire protection or thermal insulation purposes.</p></p>
