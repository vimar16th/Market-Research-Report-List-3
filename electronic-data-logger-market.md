<p><p>Electronic data loggers are devices used to monitor and record data from various sources. The market is categorized into single-channel and multi-channel loggers based on the number of inputs they can record data from. Single-channel loggers can monitor data from one source at a time, while multi-channel loggers can simultaneously monitor data from multiple sources. Single-channel loggers are suitable for simple applications, while multi-channel loggers are ideal for complex systems where data from multiple sources needs to be monitored simultaneously.</p></p>
