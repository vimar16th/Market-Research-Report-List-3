<p><p>Automotive Suspension System Market includes Independent Automotive Suspension System and Non-independent Automotive Suspension System. Independent Automotive Suspension System allows each wheel to move independently, providing better handling and comfort. This type is commonly used in high-performance vehicles. Non-independent Automotive Suspension System connects both wheels on the same axle, offering a simpler and more cost-effective solution. This type is typically found in trucks and SUVs where off-road capabilities and durability are prioritized over precise handling. Both types cater to different vehicle needs and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
