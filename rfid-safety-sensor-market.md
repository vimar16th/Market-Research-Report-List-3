<p><strong>The RFID Safety Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact RFID safety sensors require physical contact between the sensor and the object being detected, providing accurate and reliable data but may cause wear and tear over time. Non-contact RFID safety sensors use proximity technology to detect objects without physical connection, allowing for longer sensor lifespan and reduced maintenance needs. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the application, with contact sensors being ideal for precise measurements and non-contact sensors offering convenience and durability.</p></p>
