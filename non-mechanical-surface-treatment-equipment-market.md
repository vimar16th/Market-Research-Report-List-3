<p><p>Non-Mechanical Surface Treatment Equipment market includes technologies such as Plasma Treatment and Corona Treatment. Plasma Treatment involves using ionized gas to clean, activate, or coat surfaces with improved adhesion properties. Corona Treatment uses high-voltage discharge to modify surfaces for better adhesion of inks, adhesives, or coatings. Both technologies are used in various industries such as automotive, electronics, and packaging to enhance surface properties and improve product performance. These treatments offer environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional mechanical surface treatments.</p></p>
