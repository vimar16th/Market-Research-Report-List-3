<p><p>Non Biodegradable Recycled Thermoplastics Market Types are categorized into Flakes, Pellets, and Granulates. Flakes are small pieces of thermoplastics that are used in various applications such as packaging and construction. Pellets are small spherical beads of recycled thermoplastics that are melted down to create new products. Granulates are bigger particles that can be used in injection molding and extrusion processes. Each type plays a crucial role in the recycling industry by providing sustainable solutions for plastic waste.</p></p>
