<p><p>Rotary Variable Differential Transformers (RVDT) are electro-mechanical devices used for measuring angular displacement. They operate on the principle of electromagnetic induction, where the relative movement of a rotating shaft and a transformer assembly generates an electrical output proportional to the angular position. RVDTs are widely used in aerospace, industrial automation, robotics, and automotive applications due to their high accuracy and reliability.</p><p>The Rotary Variable Differential Transformers RVDT Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.60% during the forecast period. The market growth is driven by the increasing demand for precision measurement devices in various industries. RVDTs offer advantages such as high resolution, long-term stability, and resistance to harsh operating conditions, making them preferred choice for angle measurement applications.</p><p>One of the key trends in the RVDT market is the integration of advanced technologies such as digital signal processing and wireless communication capabilities, which enhance the performance and functionality of these devices. Overall, the RVDT market is expected to witness steady growth in the coming years, driven by the expansion of industries requiring precise angular measurements.</p></p>
