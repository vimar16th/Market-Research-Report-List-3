<p><ul><li>Public Cloud</li><li>Private Cloud</li><li>Hybrid Cloud</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for cloud contact centers can be categorized into three types: public cloud, private cloud, and hybrid cloud. In the public cloud market, contact center resources are hosted and managed by third-party service providers, allowing businesses to access them over the internet. Private cloud refers to contact centers that are deployed within an organization's own infrastructure, offering more control and security but requiring higher investments. Hybrid cloud combines both public and private cloud elements, providing flexibility and scalability by allowing businesses to integrate both types as per their requirements.</p></p>
