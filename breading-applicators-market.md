<p><p>Breading applicators come in two main market types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic breading applicators are fully automated machines that require minimal human intervention. They are equipped with advanced technology to efficiently coat products with breading. On the other hand, semi-automatic breading applicators are partially automated and involve more manual labor.
