<p><strong>The Automated Blood Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-part Differential Automated Blood Analyzer</li><li>Three-part Differential Automated Blood Analyzer</li><li>Five-part Differential Automated Blood Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automated blood analyzer market is categorized into three types: two-part differential, three-part differential, and five-part differential analyzers. </p><p>A two-part differential analyzer provides white blood cell (WBC) and red blood cell (RBC) counts and calculates the WBC percentage. </p><p>A three-part differential analyzer provides WBC and RBC counts along with differential white blood cell percentages (basophils, eosinophils, lymphocytes, monocytes, and neutrophils). </p><p>A five-part differential analyzer provides a detailed breakdown of WBC subtypes, offering a more comprehensive analysis of blood components and aiding in diagnosing specific diseases.</p></p>
