<p><p>The composite autoclave repair market can be divided into three main types: structural, semi-structural, and cosmetic. Structural repairs focus on fixing major damages that can compromise the integrity of the composite material, such as cracks or delamination. Semi-structural repairs address medium-level damages that don't have a significant impact on the overall structure but still need to be fixed to ensure optimal performance. Cosmetic repairs, on the other hand, deal with minor damages, mainly aimed at improving the appearance of the composite material. Each market type caters to specific repair needs.</p></p>
