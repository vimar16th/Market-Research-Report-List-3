<p><p>Glass Mat Thermoplastics (GMT) are composite materials that combine glass fibers with a thermoplastic resin matrix. There are different types of GMT market based on the type of thermoplastic used. Polypropylene (PP) GMT is known for its high impact strength and chemical resistance. Polyamide (PA) GMT offers excellent heat resistance and dimensional stability. Other types of GMT may include blends of various thermoplastics to achieve specific properties such as improved stiffness, impact resistance, or thermal performance.</p></p>
