<p><p>Particulate reinforced composites are materials made by combining a matrix material with particles to improve its mechanical properties. Two common types of particulate reinforced composites are large particle composites and dispersion strengthened composites. Large particle composites incorporate relatively large particles into the matrix, providing strength and toughness. Dispersion strengthened composites involve dispersing small particles throughout the matrix to enhance its mechanical properties. Both types of composites offer unique benefits for various applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
