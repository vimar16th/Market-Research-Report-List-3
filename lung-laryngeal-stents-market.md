<p><p>Lung Laryngeal Stents are medical devices used to keep the airways open in patients suffering from airway obstruction. There are three main types of stents available in the market: Self-expandable stents, which expand on their own once placed in the airway; Balloon-expandable stents, which are expanded using a balloon catheter; and Non-expandable stents, which are pre-shaped and do not expand further. Each type of stent offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the patient.</p></p>
