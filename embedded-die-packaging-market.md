<p><p>Embedded Die Packaging involves embedding discrete semiconductor die directly into a substrate or board, enabling smaller form factors and improved performance. The types include Embedded Die in Rigid Board, where die are embedded in a rigid PCB; Embedded Die in Flexible Board, where die are embedded in a flexible PCB; and Embedded Die in IC Package Substrate, where die are embedded in the substrate of an IC package. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of size, performance, and flexibility in various applications.</p></p>
