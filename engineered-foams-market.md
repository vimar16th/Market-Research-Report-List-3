<p><p>Engineered foams refer to materials that are designed and manufactured to have specific properties and characteristics for various applications. There are three main types of engineered foams in the market: flexible foams, rigid foams, and spray foams. </p><p>Flexible foams are soft and pliable, making them ideal for cushioning and packaging. Rigid foams are stiff and provide structural support, commonly used in construction and insulation. Spray foams are applied as a liquid and expand to fill gaps, often used for insulation and sealing purposes.</p></p>
