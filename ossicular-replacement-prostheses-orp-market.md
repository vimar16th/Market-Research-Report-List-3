<p><p>Ossicular Replacement Prostheses (ORPs) are devices used to surgically replace damaged or missing ossicles in the middle ear to restore hearing. There are two main types: Partial Ossicular Replacement Prostheses (PORPs) and Total Ossicular Replacement Prostheses (TORPs). PORPs involve replacing one or more ossicles, while TORPs involve replacing all three ossicles. These devices help conduct sound vibrations from the eardrum to the inner ear, improving hearing for individuals with conductive hearing loss.</p></p>
