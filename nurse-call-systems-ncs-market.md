Wired Communication NCS, on the other hand, rely on physical connections, like wires and cables, to transmit calls. While wireless systems offer more freedom of movement for nurses, wired systems guarantee a more stable and secure communication channel. Both options aim to improve patient care and response time in healthcare settings.</p></p>
