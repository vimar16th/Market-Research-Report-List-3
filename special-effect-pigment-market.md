<p><p>Special Effect Pigments market is divided into three main types: Metallic Pigment, Pearlescent Pigment, and Others. Metallic Pigments are made of metal flakes that provide a shiny, metallic finish to coatings and paints. Pearlescent Pigments, on the other hand, contain natural or synthetic mica particles that produce a pearl-like effect with various colors. The Others category includes a wide range of pigments such as fluorescent, phosphorescent, and thermochromic pigments that offer unique visual effects to the products they are used in.</p></p>
