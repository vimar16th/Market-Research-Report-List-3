<p><strong>The Mini LED Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lateral Chip LED</li><li>Vertical Chip LED</li><li>Flip Chip LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mini LED Chips market can be classified into three types based on the chip design: Lateral Chip LEDs, Vertical Chip LEDs, and Flip Chip LEDs. Lateral Chip LEDs have horizontal connections within the chip, offering good thermal performance. Vertical Chip LEDs have vertical connections, allowing for higher power output. Flip Chip LEDs have no wire bonding, resulting in improved heat dissipation and better electrical performance. Each type caters to different applications and requirements in the growing Mini LED market.</p></p>
