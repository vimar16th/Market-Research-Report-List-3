<p><p>Sterile dental needles come in various types, including 25G, 27G, 30G, 31G, and others. These numbers refer to the gauge size, indicating the thickness of the needle. A lower gauge number corresponds to a thicker needle, while a higher number indicates a thinner needle. The choice of gauge depends on the dental procedure being performed and the comfort level of the patient. Dental professionals may opt for larger gauge needles for more invasive procedures, while smaller gauge needles are suitable for less invasive procedures or patients with needle anxiety.</p></p>
