<p><p>Roofing membrane materials market encompasses various types such as TPO (Thermoplastic Olefin), PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer), and Modified Bitumen. TPO membranes are known for their durability and energy efficiency. PVC membranes offer great flexibility and resistance to chemicals. EPDM membranes are popular for their long lifespan and low maintenance requirements. Modified bitumen membranes are ideal for flat or low-sloped roofs due to their ability to withstand harsh weather conditions. Each type caters to different roofing needs and preferences in the construction industry.</p></p>
