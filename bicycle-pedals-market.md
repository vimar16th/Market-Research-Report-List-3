<p><p>Clipless pedals are designed for maximum efficiency and power transfer, popular among road and mountain bike riders. They feature a locking mechanism that attaches the rider's shoe to the pedal for a secure connection. On the other hand, flat pedals have a larger surface area for more foot support and are popular among casual riders and downhill mountain bikers. These pedals allow the rider to easily remove their foot from the pedal, offering more freedom and control in technical terrain. Both pedal types cater to different riding styles and preferences.</p></p>
