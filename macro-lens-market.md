<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Macro Lens market can be classified into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I refers to macro lenses with a fixed focal length, typically ranging from 50mm to 100mm. These lenses are ideal for capturing small objects and details with a 1:1 magnification ratio. On the other hand, Type II macro lenses are zoom lenses that offer flexibility in focal length, ranging from around 70mm to 200mm. This type allows photographers to capture macro shots from a distance, making it suitable for subjects that may be easily disturbed or dangerous to approach.</p></p>
