<p><p>The Food-Grade Diaphragm Pump Market consists of two main types: Air Operated Diaphragm Pumps (AODD) and Electric Driven Diaphragm Pumps (EODD). AODDs are powered by compressed air, making them suitable for hazardous environments or where electricity is unavailable. EODDs, on the other hand, are powered by electricity, providing a more consistent and controlled pumping solution. Both types are commonly used in the food industry due to their hygienic design and ability to handle viscous or abrasive materials.</p></p>
