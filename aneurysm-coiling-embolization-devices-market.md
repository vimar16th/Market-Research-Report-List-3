<p><p>Aneurysm coiling & embolization devices market includes embolic coils, flow diversion devices, and liquid embolic agents. Embolic coils are used to fill an aneurysm to prevent blood from entering and causing a rupture. Flow diversion devices redirect blood flow away from the aneurysm. Liquid embolic agents are injected into the aneurysm to block blood flow. These devices are important in treating both ruptured and unruptured aneurysms, reducing the risk of complications and potentially saving lives.</p></p>
