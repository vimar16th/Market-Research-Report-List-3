<p><p>DC isolators are essential components in electrical systems to ensure safety and prevent electric shocks. Single break isolators have one isolating point, while double break isolators have two isolating points for added safety. Pantograph type isolators have a sliding mechanism for easy operation. Each type of isolator caters to specific requirements and is used in various applications such as solar power systems, battery storage systems, and industrial equipment to safely isolate power sources during maintenance or emergencies.</p></p>
