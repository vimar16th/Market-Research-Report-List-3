<p><p>Vertical-cavity surface emitting lasers (VCSELs) are classified into two main types: single-mode VCSELs and multimode VCSELs. Single-mode VCSELs are designed for applications requiring high beam quality and precise control of the emitted light, making them suitable for data communication and sensing applications. On the other hand, multimode VCSELs are used in applications where a broader beam is required, such as laser printing and optical sensing. Each type caters to specific market needs and offers distinct advantages for different applications within the optical communication industry.</p></p>
