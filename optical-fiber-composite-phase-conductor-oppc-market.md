<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Composite Phase Conductor (OPPC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure OPPC</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPPC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Fiber Composite Phase Conductor (OPPC) comes in two main types: Central Tube Structure OPPC and Layer Stranding Structure OPPC. Central Tube Structure OPPC consists of optical fibers housed in a central tube, providing protection and flexibility. Layer Stranding Structure OPPC has the optical fibers placed between layers of aluminum strands, offering strength and stability. Both types of OPPC are widely used in power transmission and distribution systems due to their high capacity for data transmission and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.</p></p>
