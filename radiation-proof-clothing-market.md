Polyion type clothing contains polyionic compounds that can shield against radiation. Metal fiber type clothing consists of metal fibers interwoven into the fabric, which forms a barrier against radiation. The "Others" category includes clothing made from alternative technologies or materials not fitting into the previous types. These categories encompass different techniques and materials used to create radiation-proof clothing, ensuring safety in various environments.</p></p>
