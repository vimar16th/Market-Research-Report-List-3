<p><p>There are two main types of lead-acid batteries in the market: VRLA (valve-regulated lead-acid) batteries and Flooded batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed and maintenance-free, making them ideal for applications where minimal maintenance is required. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, require regular maintenance and topping up with distilled water. VRLA batteries are commonly used in UPS systems, telecommunications, and renewable energy applications, while flooded batteries are more commonly used in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
