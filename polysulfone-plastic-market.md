<p><p>Polysulfone plastics, including PSU (polysulfone), PES (polyethersulfone), and PPSU (polypheylenesulfone), are high-performance engineering thermoplastics known for their excellent heat resistance, chemical resistance, and mechanical properties. PSU is commonly used in medical devices, automotive parts, and plumbing fixtures. PES is often used in aerospace, electronics, and automotive applications. PPSU is popular in food service, medical, and plumbing industries due to its superior toughness and hydrolytic stability. These three types of polysulfone plastics cater to a wide range of industrial needs.</p></p>
