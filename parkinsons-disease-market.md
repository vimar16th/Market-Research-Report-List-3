<p><p>Parkinson's Disease market can be categorized into three main types of medications: Carbidopa/Levodopa, Dopamine Receptor Agonists, and MAO-Inhibitors. Carbidopa/Levodopa works by increasing dopamine levels in the brain to improve movement symptoms. Dopamine Receptor Agonists mimic the effects of dopamine in the brain to help control movement. MAO-Inhibitors prevent the breakdown of dopamine, also helping to regulate movement. These medications provide different approaches to managing Parkinson's symptoms and are often used in combination for optimal treatment.</p></p>
