<p><p>Off-Highway Hybrid Vehicles Market offers different types of hybrid technology such as Series Hybrid and Parallel Hybrid. In a Series Hybrid system, the engine only generates power for the electric motor, which drives the vehicle. This setup is more fuel-efficient as the engine operates at its optimal speed. On the other hand, Parallel Hybrid vehicles use both the engine and electric motor to drive the wheels simultaneously, providing a balance between power and efficiency. These market types cater to different needs and preferences of consumers in the off-highway vehicle segment.</p></p>
