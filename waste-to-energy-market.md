<p><p>Waste-to-Energy market involves two main types of technologies: Thermal and Biochemical. Thermal technologies convert waste materials into heat, electricity, or fuel through processes like incineration, gasification, and pyrolysis. Biochemical reactions, on the other hand, involve the use of microorganisms to break down organic waste into biogas or biofuels. Both types of technologies provide sustainable solutions for managing waste while generating energy, reducing dependency on fossil fuels, and mitigating environmental impacts associated with waste disposal.</p></p>
