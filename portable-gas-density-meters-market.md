<p><p>Portable Gas Density Meters are available in two main types - In-line Gas Density Meter and Desktop Gas Density Meter. In-line Gas Density Meters are mounted directly in the process line to continuously measure the gas density. They are ideal for applications where real-time density monitoring is required. On the other hand, Desktop Gas Density Meters are standalone devices that are typically used in laboratory or research settings for occasional gas density measurements. Both types offer accurate and reliable density measurement capabilities for various industrial applications.</p></p>
