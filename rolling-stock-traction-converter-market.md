<p><p>Rolling Stock Traction Converter market comprises of two main types: Propulsion Converter and Integrated Compact Converter. Propulsion converters are responsible for converting electrical energy into mechanical energy to drive the train's wheels. On the other hand, Integrated Compact Converters combine various functions such as traction, auxiliary power supply, and battery charging in a single compact unit. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and reliable operation of rolling stock by providing the necessary power conversion and control functionalities.</p></p>
