<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Active Power Steering Systems market can be categorized into three main types: Electric Power Steering (EPS), Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), and Other. EPS systems use an electric motor to assist with steering, offering improved fuel efficiency and flexibility. EHPS systems combine hydraulic fluid with an electric pump to provide steering assistance, offering a balance between hydraulic and electric systems. Other market types may include innovative steering technologies not classified under EPS or EHPS, such as advanced automated steering systems.</p></p>
