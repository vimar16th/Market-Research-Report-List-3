<p><p>LCP Flexible Copper Clad Laminate (FCCL) and MPI Flexible Copper Clad Laminate (FCCL) are two types of flexible copper clad laminates commonly used in electronic devices. LCP FCCL is popular for its high thermal stability and excellent chemical resistance. On the other hand, MPI FCCL is known for its low dielectric constant and low water absorption rate. Both types cater to different market needs, with LCP FCCL being preferred for high-tech applications and MPI FCCL for high-frequency and high-speed performance requirements.</p></p>
