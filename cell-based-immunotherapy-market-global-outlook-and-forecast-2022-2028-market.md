<p><p>Cell-Based Immunotherapy is a cutting-edge approach to treating various diseases, including cancer and autoimmune disorders. The market is segmented into three main types: Autologous Cellular Immunotherapy, Chimeric Antigen Receptor (CAR) T-Cell Therapy, and Dendritic Cell-based Vaccine Therapy. Autologous Cellular Immunotherapy involves using a patient's own cells to target and destroy cancer cells. CAR T-Cell Therapy involves genetically modifying a patient's T-cells to recognize and attack cancer cells. Dendritic Cell-based Vaccine Therapy utilizes dendritic cells to stimulate the immune system to target cancer cells. The global market for Cell-Based Immunotherapy is expected to see significant growth in the forecast period of 2022-2028.</p></p>
