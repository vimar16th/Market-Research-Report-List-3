<p><p>Paper mass silica market types include precipitated silica, fumed silica, colloidal silica, and others. Precipitated silica is produced by the neutralization of sodium silicate with sulfuric acid. Fumed silica is a fine, white powder made by burning silicon tetrachloride in a flame of hydrogen and oxygen. Colloidal silica consists of very fine particles suspended in a liquid. Other types of silica in the market may include silica gel or quartz powder, each with unique properties and applications in the paper industry.</p></p>
