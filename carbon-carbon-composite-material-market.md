<p><p>Carbon-Carbon Composite Material Market includes two main types - Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Liquid Impregnation Process. </p><p>CVD involves depositing carbon atoms onto a substrate through a chemical reaction, resulting in a high-strength material ideal for aerospace and defense applications. </p><p>Liquid Impregnation Process involves immersing preform materials in a liquid matrix, allowing for customization and increased flexibility in shaping the final product. Both methods offer unique advantages in terms of strength, durability, and application versatility in various industries.</p></p>
