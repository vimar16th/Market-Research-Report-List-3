<p><strong>The Aircraft Tow Tugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Towbarless Tractors</li><li>Conventional Tractors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft tow tugs market consists of two primary types: towbarless tractors and conventional tractors. Towbarless tractors utilize a lifting mechanism to attach to the nose landing gear of the aircraft, eliminating the need for a towbar. This results in faster and more efficient towing operations. On the other hand, conventional tractors use a towbar to connect to the aircraft, providing stability and control during towing. Both types of tugs cater to different needs and preferences in the aviation industry.</p></p>
