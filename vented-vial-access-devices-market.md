<p><p>Vented vial access devices are a type of medical equipment designed to facilitate the sterile transfer of drug products between vials. These devices consist of a rubber or silicone septum, which can be pierced by a needle or syringe for easy extraction or injection of the medication. Vented vial access devices also contain a built-in filter that allows for the equalization of pressure during drug withdrawal or infusion, preventing the formation of aerosols or spills.</p><p>The vented vial access devices market is projected to witness substantial growth in the coming years.
