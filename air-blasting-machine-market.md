<p><ul><li>Gravity Suction Type</li><li>Direct Pressure Type</li><li>Blower Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air blasting machines are used for various applications like cleaning, smoothing, or roughening surfaces. These machines are categorized into three types: Gravity Suction Type, Direct Pressure Type, and Blower Type. </p><p>Gravity Suction Type machines operate by using negative pressure to draw abrasive material into the air stream, while Direct Pressure Type machines use positive pressure to propel the abrasive material onto the surface. Blower Type machines use a high-velocity airflow to propel the abrasive material. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different industrial applications.</p></p>
