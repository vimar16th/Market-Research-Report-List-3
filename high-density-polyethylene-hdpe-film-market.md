<p><p>High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) Film market is segmented into three main types: Translucent HDPE Film, Opaque HDPE Film, and Others. Translucent HDPE Film is semi-transparent and allows some light to pass through, commonly used for packaging applications. Opaque HDPE Film is completely non-transparent, often used for applications requiring light blockage or UV protection. The "Others" category includes specialty HDPE films with unique properties or applications. Each type caters to specific needs in industries such as packaging, agriculture, construction, and healthcare.</p></p>
