<p><p>Surveying receivers can be categorized into two main market types: Handheld GNSS Receivers and Fixed GNSS Receivers. Handheld GNSS receivers are portable devices used for mapping and data collection in the field. They are commonly used for tasks such as boundary marking and asset inventory. On the other hand, Fixed GNSS receivers are stationary devices typically installed on buildings or structures to provide precise and continuous positioning data. They are commonly used in construction, monitoring, and scientific applications.</p></p>
