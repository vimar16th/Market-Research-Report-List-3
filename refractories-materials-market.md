Acidic refractories are resistant to acidic environments, neutral refractories are resistant to both acidic and basic environments, while basic refractories are resistant to basic environments. Each type of refractory material is suited for specific industrial applications depending on the chemical and thermal conditions it will be exposed to. Understanding the characteristics and capabilities of each type is crucial in selecting the right refractory material for a particular industrial process.</p></p>
