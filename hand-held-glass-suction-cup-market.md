<p><ul><li>Manual Suction Cup</li><li>Automatic Suction Cup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hand-held glass suction cups are primarily divided into two types: manual suction cups and automatic suction cups. Manual suction cups require physical force to create a vacuum seal between the cup and the surface, allowing for lifting and moving of heavy glass objects. On the other hand, automatic suction cups have powered mechanisms that create the vacuum seal with a push of a button, making the lifting process easier and more efficient. Both types cater to different user preferences and specific requirements in the market.</p></p>
