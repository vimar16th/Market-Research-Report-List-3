<p><strong>The Wafer Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tape for Grinding</li><li>Tape for Dicing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wafer tape for grinding is used in the semiconductor industry during the wafer thinning process to secure the wafer and prevent damage during grinding. On the other hand, wafer tape for dicing is used during the dicing process to hold the individual die in place and protect them from any potential damage. Both types of wafer tape play a crucial role in ensuring the integrity of the wafers and die throughout the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
