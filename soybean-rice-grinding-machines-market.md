<p><p>Soybean rice grinding machines are available in two main types - wet grinding machines and dry grinding machines. Wet grinding machines are equipped with water-cooled grinder stones that grind soaked soybeans and rice into a smooth paste, ideal for making traditional Asian dishes like soy milk and rice cakes. On the other hand, dry grinding machines are designed to grind dry ingredients like roasted soybeans and rice into a fine powder, perfect for making dry mixes and flours for baking and cooking.</p></p>
