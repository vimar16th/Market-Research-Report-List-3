<p><p>A Silicon Controlled Rectifier (SCR) is a type of semiconductor device used for power control in electronic devices. There are two main types of SCR markets: Unidirectional SCR and Bidirectional SCR. Unidirectional SCRs conduct current in only one direction, making them suitable for applications that require only forward conduction. Bidirectional SCRs, on the other hand, can conduct current in both directions, allowing for bidirectional power control. Both types of SCRs play important roles in various electronic systems, providing efficient and reliable power regulation.</p></p>
