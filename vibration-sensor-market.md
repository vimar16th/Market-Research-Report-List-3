<p><strong>The Vibration Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoresistive</li><li>Strain Gauge</li><li>Variable Capacitance</li><li>Tri-Axial Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vibration sensors are classified into different types based on their operating principles. Piezoresistive sensors measure resistance changes in response to vibrations. Strain gauge sensors detect strain in a material caused by vibrations. Variable capacitance sensors measure changes in capacitance induced by vibrations. Tri-axial sensors can detect vibrations in three dimensions. Each type of sensor offers unique benefits and is suitable for different applications depending on the level of sensitivity and accuracy required for monitoring vibrations in various industries.</p></p>
