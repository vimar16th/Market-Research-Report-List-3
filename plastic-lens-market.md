<p><p>Plastic Lens market consists of two main types: Aspheric Lens and Spherical Lens. Aspheric Lens are designed with non-spherical surfaces, providing better image quality and reducing aberrations. These lenses are commonly used in cameras, telescopes, and eyeglasses. Spherical Lens have curved surfaces with the same radius, making them simpler and cheaper to manufacture compared to aspheric lenses. They are commonly used in applications where precise imaging is not critical, such as simple magnifying glasses.</p></p>
