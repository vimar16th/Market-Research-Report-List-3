<p><p>Copper-based wood preservatives, such as Alkaline Copper Quaternary (ACQ), Micronized Copper Azole (MCA), and Copper Azole (CA), are used to protect wood from decay and insect damage. ACQ is a water-based preservative, MCA is a finely ground copper additive, and CA is a combination of copper and organic azoles. Each type offers different levels of protection and application methods, making them suitable for various wood preservation needs in construction and landscaping projects.</p></p>
