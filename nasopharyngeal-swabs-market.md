<p><p>Nasopharyngeal swabs used in medical procedures can be classified into two types based on the materials they are made of: polyethylene (PE) swabs and polypropylene (PP) swabs. PE is a lightweight, flexible material that is resistant to moisture and chemicals, making it suitable for single-use swabs. PP, on the other hand, is a more rigid and durable plastic that can endure multiple uses and sterilization processes. The choice of PE or PP swabs depends on factors like intended use, required flexibility, and cost considerations.</p></p>
