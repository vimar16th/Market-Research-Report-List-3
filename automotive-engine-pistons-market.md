<p><ul><li>Trunk Pistons</li><li>Crosshead Pistons</li><li>Slipper Pistons</li><li>Deflector Pistons</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive engine pistons come in various types based on their design and functionality. Trunk pistons have a cylindrical body with a flat head, while crosshead pistons have a projecting arm attached to the piston head. Slipper pistons have a skirt that partially covers the piston pin. Deflector pistons have a specially shaped head to direct airflow for improved combustion efficiency. Each type of piston has unique characteristics and is used in different engine designs to optimize performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
