<p><p>Thermoforming films are categorized into two main types: rigid thermoforming films and flexible thermoforming films. Rigid thermoforming films are typically used for packaging products that require a higher level of protection and stability, such as electronics or medical devices. On the other hand, flexible thermoforming films are more commonly used for packaging products that need to be easily accessible or have irregular shapes, such as food or pharmaceutical items. Both types of films are extensively used in various industries for their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
