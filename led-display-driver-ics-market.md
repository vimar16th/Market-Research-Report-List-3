<p><ul><li>AC/DC LED Lighting Driver IC</li><li>DC/DC LED Lighting Driver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED Display Driver ICs market can be categorized into two main types: AC/DC LED Lighting Driver IC and DC/DC LED Lighting Driver IC. AC/DC LED Lighting Driver ICs are designed to convert AC voltage into DC voltage, making them suitable for applications where AC power source is used. On the other hand, DC/DC LED Lighting Driver ICs are used to regulate and control the DC voltage supplied to the LEDs, making them ideal for applications where DC power source is required. Both types of ICs play a vital role in driving and controlling LED lighting systems.</p></p>
