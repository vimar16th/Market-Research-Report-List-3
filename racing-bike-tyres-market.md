<p><p>Racing bike tyres come in three main types - clincher, tubular, and tubeless. Clincher bike tyres are the most common and are easy to install and repair. Tubular bike tyres are lighter and provide a smoother ride, but they can be more challenging to maintain. Tubeless bike tyres offer the advantage of reduced rolling resistance and the ability to run at lower pressures for improved comfort and traction. Each type caters to different preferences and riding styles in the racing bike market.</p></p>
