<p><p>Soft touch polyurethane coatings can be divided into two main types based on their formulation: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly and contain lower levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them a popular choice for manufacturers looking to reduce their environmental impact. On the other hand, solvent-based coatings offer advantages such as faster drying times and better resistance to chemicals and abrasion. Both types of coatings have their own set of benefits and are used in a variety of industries for different applications.</p></p>
