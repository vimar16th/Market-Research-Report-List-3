<p><p>Drop On Demand (DOD) Inkjet Inks are utilized in two main types of inkjet printing technologies: Thermal DOD and Piezoelectric DOD. Thermal DOD technology uses heat to create drops of ink, while Piezoelectric DOD technology uses an electrically charged crystal to produce drops of ink. Both technologies offer high precision and reliability in inkjet printing, making them popular choices for a variety of applications including graphic design, packaging, textiles, and industrial printing.
