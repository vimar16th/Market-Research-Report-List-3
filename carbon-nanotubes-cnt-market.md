<p><p>Carbon nanotubes (CNT) are cylindrical carbon structures with remarkable properties that make them highly sought after in various industries. There are two main types of CNT markets: Multi-Walled Carbon Nanotubes (MWCNT) and Single-Walled Carbon Nanotubes (SWCNT). MWCNT are composed of several layers of graphene sheets, offering high strength and electrical conductivity. On the other hand, SWCNT consist of a single layer of graphene, providing exceptional mechanical and electrical properties. Both types of CNT have diverse applications in electronics, aerospace, medicine, and other sectors.</p></p>
