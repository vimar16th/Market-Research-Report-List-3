<p><p>The Digital MEMS Microphone market types refer to different interfaces for connecting microphones to electronic devices. </p><p>I2S Interface enables direct digital communication between the microphone and the device. TDM Interface supports multiple microphones connected through one input/output line. PDM Interface converts analog sound waves into digital pulses for transmission. </p><p>Each interface type has its own advantages and applications. I2S is preferred for high-quality audio recording, TDM for multi-microphone setups, and PDM for energy-efficient and cost-effective solutions.</p></p>
