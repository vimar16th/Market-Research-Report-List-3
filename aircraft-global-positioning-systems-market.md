<p><p>The Aircraft Global Positioning Systems market is divided into two main types: Portable GPS and Fixed GPS. Fixed GPS systems, on the other hand, are permanently installed in aircraft and provide continuous, reliable positioning information. These systems are typically more advanced and integrated into the aircraft's overall navigation system.</p></p>
