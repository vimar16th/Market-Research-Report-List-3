<p><p>Biostimulants market can be divided into different types, including Acid-based Biostimulants, Plant Extract Biostimulants, and Others. Plant Extract biostimulants are made from natural sources like seaweed, amino acids, and other plant extracts to promote plant growth and improve stress resistance. Other types of biostimulants may include microbial-based products or nutrient-rich formulations with specific benefits for plants.</p></p>
