<p><p>Motor monitoring service refers to the practice of monitoring and analyzing various parameters related to motors in order to track their performance, detect any potential faults or failures, and optimize their operation. One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing adoption of motor monitoring systems across various industries, such as manufacturing, oil and gas, power generation, and mining, among others. Motor monitoring services help in optimizing motor performance, thereby increasing energy efficiency and extending motor lifespan.
