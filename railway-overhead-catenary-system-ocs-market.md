<p><p>The Railway Overhead Catenary System (OCS) is a vital component of electrified railways, which supplies electrical power to trains. It consists of a series of overhead wires, also known as catenaries, that are supported by structures called supports or rigid brackets. These wires carry electrical power from substations to trains, enabling them to run on electricity rather than fossil fuels. The OCS ensures reliable power transmission, reduced operating costs, and improved environmental performance.</p><p>The Railway Overhead Catenary System (OCS) Market is anticipated to witness significant growth during the forecast period. <p><p>The Railway Overhead Catenary System (OCS) refers to the electrical supply system used by trains that run on electric power. The OCS market can be classified into two types: flexible and rigid. In the flexible market, the catenary wires are more elastic, allowing for slight variations in train movements and providing better adaptability to different conditions. In contrast, the rigid market involves stiffer catenary wires, which are more stable and suited for high-speed trains. Both flexible and rigid OCS markets offer specific advantages depending on the train's operating conditions and requirements.</p></p>
