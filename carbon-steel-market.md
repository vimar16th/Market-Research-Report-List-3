<p><p>Carbon steel is categorized into three main types based on its carbon content: low carbon steel (up to 0.3% carbon), medium carbon steel (0.3% - 0.6% carbon), and high carbon steel (more than 0.6% carbon). Low carbon steel is known for its weldability and ductility, while medium carbon steel offers good strength and hardness. High carbon steel is the strongest but can be brittle. Each type of carbon steel has its own unique properties and is used in various industries for different applications.</p></p>
