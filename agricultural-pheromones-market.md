<p><p>Agricultural pheromones are classified into different types based on their functions. Sex pheromones are emitted by insects to attract mates, while aggregation pheromones are used to gather individuals of the same species together. Other types of pheromones in the agricultural market include alarm pheromones that warn others of potential threats, and trail pheromones that guide group movement. Each type plays a crucial role in pest control and crop management strategies in agriculture.</p></p>
