Chemotherapy involves using drugs to kill cancer cells throughout the body. Radiation therapy uses high-energy beams to target and destroy cancer cells. Splenectomy involves surgical removal of the spleen, which can be enlarged in CML. Stem cell transplant involves replacing diseased bone marrow with healthy stem cells to promote new blood cell growth.</p></p>
