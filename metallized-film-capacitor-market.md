<p><strong>The Metallized Film Capacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC</li><li>DC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC metallized film capacitors are designed for use in alternating current circuits, where the voltage periodically changes direction. These capacitors are typically used in applications such as motors, power supplies, and lighting ballasts. On the other hand, DC metallized film capacitors are specifically designed for use in direct current circuits, where the voltage remains constant. They are commonly used in applications such as automotive electronics, telecommunications, and consumer electronics. Both types of capacitors play a crucial role in filtering, smoothing, and storing electrical energy in various electronic devices.</p></p>
