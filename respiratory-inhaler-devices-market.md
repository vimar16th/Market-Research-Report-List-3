<p><p>Respiratory Inhaler Devices Market includes three main types: Dry Powder Inhaler (DPI), Metered Dose Inhaler (MDI), and Nebulizer. DPIs deliver medication in dry powder form and are breath-activated. MDIs deliver a measured dose of medication in aerosol form with the help of a propellant. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into a fine mist for inhalation. Each type has its own benefits and drawbacks, catering to different patient needs and preferences in the treatment of respiratory conditions like asthma and COPD.</p></p>
