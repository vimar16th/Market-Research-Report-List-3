<p><p>The Material Handling Overhead Cranes market can be categorized into two main types: Single-Girder Overhead Cranes and Double-Girder Overhead Cranes. Single-Girder Overhead Cranes consist of a single bridge girder supported on two end trucks, while Double-Girder Overhead Cranes have two bridge girders supported on four end trucks. Single-Girder Cranes are commonly used for light to moderate lifting applications, while Double-Girder Cranes are typically used for heavier lifting requirements in industries such as construction, manufacturing, and logistics.</p></p>
