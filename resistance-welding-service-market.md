<p><p>Resistance welding service market includes various types of welding processes such as spot welding, projection welding, seam welding, butt welding, and resistance bolt welding. Spot welding is commonly used for joining overlapping metal sheets, while projection welding is used for joining multiple fasteners to a base metal. Seam welding is suitable for creating continuous joints on long metal sheets. Butt welding is used for joining two metal pieces along their edges. Resistance bolt welding is useful for joining bolts to metal plates, providing a strong and reliable connection.</p></p>
