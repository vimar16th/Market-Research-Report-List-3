<p><p>Automatic vehicle wash systems come in two main types: Semi-Automatic and Fully-Automatic. Semi-Automatic wash systems require some level of manual operation by the user, such as controlling the washing process or moving the vehicle through the system. Fully-Automatic wash systems, on the other hand, operate entirely without user intervention, using sensors and computer controls to clean vehicles automatically. Both types of systems are popular choices for car wash businesses looking to streamline their operations and improve efficiency.</p></p>
