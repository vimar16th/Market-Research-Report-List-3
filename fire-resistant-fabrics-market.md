<p><p>Fire-resistant fabrics market can be categorized into two types: Modified Flame Retardant Fiber and Essential Flame Retardant Fiber. Modified flame retardant fibers are chemically treated to enhance their fire-resistant properties, while essential flame retardant fibers are inherently resistant to flames without the need for extra treatment. Both types of fibers are crucial in industries such as construction, automotive, and firefighting where protection against fire hazards is a priority. These fabrics provide an added layer of safety for workers and consumers in potentially dangerous environments.</p></p>
