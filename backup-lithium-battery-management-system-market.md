<p><strong>The Backup Lithium Battery Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Center Type</li><li>Distribution Type</li><li>Modular Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Backup lithium battery management systems come in three main types - Center Type, Distribution Type, and Modular Type. Center Type systems have a centralized management unit that controls all batteries in a single location. Distribution Type systems have multiple management units distributed across various locations, allowing for greater flexibility and scalability. Modular Type systems are designed with individual battery modules that can be easily replaced or expanded as needed, offering customization and easy maintenance. Each type serves different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
