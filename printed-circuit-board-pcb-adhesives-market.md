<p><p>Printed Circuit Board (PCB) adhesives are used for bonding and securing components on the board to ensure electrical connections remain intact. The types of adhesives commonly used in the market are Acrylic-Based, Cyanoacrylate-Based, Epoxy-Based, Silicone-Based, and Polyurethane-Based. Acrylic-based adhesives offer good adhesion and durability, while Cyanoacrylate-based adhesives provide fast curing times. Epoxy-based adhesives are widely used for their strong bonding capabilities. Silicone-based adhesives offer flexibility and resistance to extreme temperatures. Polyurethane-based adhesives provide high strength and chemical resistance.</p></p>
