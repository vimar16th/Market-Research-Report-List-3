<p><ul><li>CMOS Type</li><li>CCD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Miniature cameras can be classified into two main types: CMOS and CCD. CMOS cameras are known for their low power consumption, making them ideal for portable devices such as smartphones. On the other hand, CCD cameras are favored for their high image quality and sensitivity in low light conditions. Both types cater to different market needs, with CMOS cameras dominating the market for applications requiring power efficiency, while CCD cameras are preferred for tasks where image quality is paramount.</p></p>
