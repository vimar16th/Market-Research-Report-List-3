<p><strong>The Resistance Bridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quarter-bridge</li><li>Half-bridge</li><li>Full-bridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Resistance bridge market types include quarter-bridge, half-bridge, and full-bridge configurations. In a quarter-bridge, only one arm of the bridge is shunted by a strain gauge, limiting sensitivity. A half-bridge utilizes two arms for improved sensitivity but requires external balancing. A full-bridge incorporates all four arms for maximum sensitivity and accuracy. Each configuration offers different levels of precision and complexity, catering to diverse applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and structural monitoring.</p></p>
