<p><p>Air Volume Controllers are devices used in HVAC systems to control the amount of air flow in a building. There are two main types of Air Volume Controllers - Variable Air Volume Controller (VAV) and Constant Air Volume Controller (CAV). VAV controllers adjust the air flow based on the heating or cooling needs of different zones, while CAV controllers maintain a constant air flow regardless of the zone's requirements. Both types of controllers help in achieving energy efficiency and maintaining a comfortable indoor environment.</p></p>
