<p><p>Thermal transfer print heads are classified into two main types - thick film printheads and thin film printheads. Thick film printheads are known for their durability and resistance to wear and tear, making them ideal for high-volume printing applications. On the other hand, thin film printheads are lighter and more energy-efficient, making them suitable for compact and portable printers. Both types of print heads play a crucial role in thermal transfer printing technology, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
