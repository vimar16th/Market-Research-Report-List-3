<p><p>Patterned glass is categorized into Single Roller Method and Double Roller Method markets. In the Single Roller Method market, a single roller is used to create patterns on the glass surface, resulting in a more uniform and consistent design. On the other hand, the Double Roller Method market involves two rollers creating patterns simultaneously, leading to more intricate and complex designs. Both markets cater to different customer preferences, offering a variety of patterned glass options for various applications.</p></p>
