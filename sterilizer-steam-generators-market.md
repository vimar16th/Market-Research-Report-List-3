<p><ul><li>Integral Boiler</li><li>Remote Boiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sterilizer steam generators can be classified into two main types: integral boiler and remote boiler. Integral boiler systems have the boiler connected directly to the sterilizer, providing a compact and space-saving solution. On the other hand, remote boiler systems have the boiler located separately from the sterilizer, offering flexibility in installation and maintenance. Both types of steam generators are commonly used in various industries such as healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and laboratories for sterilizing equipment and instruments.</p></p>
