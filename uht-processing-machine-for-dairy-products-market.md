<p><p>UHT processing machines for dairy products can be classified into two main types: direct and indirect. Direct UHT processing machines involve heating the dairy product directly through steam injection or heating elements. Indirect UHT processing machines heat the dairy product through a heat exchanger, where the product and heating medium do not come into direct contact. Both types of machines are used in the dairy industry to extend the shelf life of products by killing bacteria and enzymes through ultra-high temperature processing.</p></p>
