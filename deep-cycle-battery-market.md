<p><p>Deep-Cycle Battery Market Types - Flooded Deep Cycle Batteries and Sealed Deep Cycle Batteries, both cater to different needs. Flooded deep cycle batteries are ideal for applications where regular maintenance and topping up of electrolyte are possible. On the other hand, sealed deep cycle batteries are maintenance-free and can be used in confined spaces or indoor applications where ventilation is limited. These two market types offer options for users to choose the best-suited battery for their specific requirements.</p></p>
