<p><p>Vacuum hopper loaders are widely used in industries for efficiently transferring granular or powdered materials. Integral type loaders, also known as centralized loaders, are installed at a central location and use a single, powerful vacuum pump to convey materials to multiple processing machines simultaneously. Split type loaders, on the other hand, consist of separate components such as a vacuum pump and receiver, allowing for flexibility in installation and enabling customized loading solutions. Both integral and split types cater to different needs, providing efficient material transfer options in industrial settings.</p></p>
