<p><p>Shape Memory Polymer (SMP) materials are able to return to their original shape after being deformed, making them ideal for applications where shape change is needed. Liquid Crystal Elastomer (LCE) materials exhibit responsive properties to external stimuli, allowing for controlled shape transformation. Shape Memory Alloy (SMA) materials can return to a predetermined shape when subjected to a specific stimulus. Hydrogel materials are able to absorb and retain water, making them suitable for applications in drug delivery and tissue engineering.</p></p>
