<p><p>Feedthrough ceramic capacitors are categorized into DC feedthrough ceramic capacitors and AC feedthrough ceramic capacitors. DC feedthrough ceramic capacitors are designed to filter out noise in DC power lines, providing a stable voltage output. On the other hand, AC feedthrough ceramic capacitors are used to eliminate electromagnetic interference in AC power lines, ensuring a smooth and uninterrupted power supply. Both types play a crucial role in various electronic devices and systems, contributing to their efficiency and performance.</p></p>
