<p><p>Stationary battery testing equipment market can be broken down into three main types: cell testing, module testing, and pack testing. </p><p>Cell testing involves testing individual battery cells to ensure they are functioning properly. Module testing focuses on testing groups of cells that are connected together within a battery module. Pack testing involves testing the entire battery pack, which includes multiple modules. Each type of testing plays a crucial role in ensuring the overall performance and safety of stationary batteries used in various applications.</p></p>
