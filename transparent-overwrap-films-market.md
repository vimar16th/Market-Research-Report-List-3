<p><p>Transparent overwrap films are plastic films used to wrap and protect various products. Shrink films are types of overwrap films that, when exposed to heat, shrink tightly around products, providing secure packaging. Stretch films, on the other hand, are elastic films that stretch and cling to products without the need for heat. Both types of films offer transparency, allowing visibility of the packaged products. Shrink films provide a tight, tamper-resistant seal, while stretch films offer flexibility and secure bundling of multiple items.</p></p>
