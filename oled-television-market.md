<p><strong>The OLED Television Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED)</li><li>PMOLED (Passive Matrix OLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>OLED televisions are categorized into two main types - AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED) and PMOLED (Passive Matrix OLED) markets. AMOLED displays use a thin-film transistor backplane to control each individual pixel, allowing for faster response times and higher resolution. On the other hand, PMOLED displays use a simpler control scheme, making them more cost-effective but with lower performance. Both market types offer stunning picture quality and vibrant colors, but AMOLED is more commonly found in high-end televisions due to its superior technology.</p></p>
