<p><p>Plastic sterilization containers are widely used in healthcare facilities to safely store and transport sterilized medical instruments. Two common types of these containers are filter type and valve type. Filter type containers are equipped with a filtration system that allows air to circulate while preventing contaminants from entering. On the other hand, valve type containers have built-in valves that can release excess pressure during the sterilization process. These different types of containers cater to various sterilization requirements and ensure the efficient and safe storage of medical instruments.</p></p>
