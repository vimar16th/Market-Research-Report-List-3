<p><ul><li>Bituminous</li><li>Elastomeric</li><li>Cementitious</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid applied membranes are used in construction to provide waterproofing and protection against moisture penetration. There are three main types of liquid applied membranes - bituminous, elastomeric, and cementitious. Bituminous membranes are made from asphalt or bitumen and provide excellent waterproofing properties. Elastomeric membranes are made from synthetic rubber and offer flexibility and durability. Cementitious membranes are made from cement and polymers and are ideal for waterproofing concrete surfaces. Each type of liquid applied membrane has its own set of benefits and applications in the construction industry.</p></p>
