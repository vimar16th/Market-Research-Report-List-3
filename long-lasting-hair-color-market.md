<p><strong>The Long Lasting Hair Color Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Type</li><li>Semi-permanent Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Long-lasting hair color market is divided into two main types: permanent and semi-permanent. Permanent hair color penetrates the hair shaft and lasts until the hair grows out, typically requiring root touch-ups every 4-6 weeks. On the other hand, semi-permanent hair color coats the outside of the hair shaft and gradually fades with each wash, lasting around 4-6 weeks. Both types offer different levels of color longevity and commitment, catering to the diverse preferences of consumers in the hair color market.</p></p>
