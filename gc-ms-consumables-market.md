<p><p>GC-MS Consumables market includes various types of products such as Ion Source, Chromatographic Column, Filter, Injector, and others. Ion Source generates ions for the analysis, Chromatographic Column separates the components of the sample, Filter removes impurities from the sample, and Injector introduces the sample into the instrument. Other consumables may include septa, liners, and syringes. These consumables are essential for the proper functioning of a GC-MS system and are crucial for accurate and reliable analysis of samples.</p></p>
