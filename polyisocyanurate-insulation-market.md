<p><p>Polyisocyanurate insulation is available in two main types in the market: spray type and foam/board type. The spray type is applied by spraying liquid polyisocyanurate onto a surface, where it expands and hardens to provide insulation. Foam/board type comes in rigid boards or sheets that can be easily cut and installed in various applications. Both types offer excellent thermal resistance, moisture resistance, and fire resistance, making them popular choices for a variety of construction projects.</p></p>
